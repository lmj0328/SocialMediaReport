About Mirror

Mirror resembles “self-reflection”, the mindfulness that helps you identify who you are and reduce confusion about yourself in your life.

We thrive to develop this personal informatics tool to help users self reflect and gain self-knowledge on social media by allowing users to explore and track their usage patterns periodically.

Why Mirror ?

1

To find precious moments of your life

Soical media stores a lot of moments of your life: photos, new friends, or a new stage of your life. What happened?

2

To explore your usage

Social media seeminglessly blend into our life where we use it just like natural breathing. Do you want to know how often do you breathe?

3

To learn what others know about you

Social media is not only for you, but also for others to know more about you. Are you curious about what other people know about you?
